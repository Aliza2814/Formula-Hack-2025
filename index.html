About

Formula Hacks 2025 is a hackathon inspired by Formula 1, bringing together the brightest minds to innovate at the intersection of AI, Data Science, and Web3. Experience 30 hours of intense coding, collaboration, and creativity as you race to build groundbreaking solutions.

Tracks

F1 Open Theme (AI)

AI-driven simulators, analytics & dashboards for motorsport innovation.

Decentralized F1 (Web3)

NFTs, tokenized fan platforms, immutable race records on blockchain.

F1 Gaming & Experience

Games, XR experiences & gamified fan engagement platforms.

Industry Challenge

Sponsor-defined real-world problem statements to solve.
